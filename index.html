Georgia Football

By Peter Fredella

The Dawgs may not look amazing at the moment, but there is potential for this team to bring home some hardware in the near future.

Kirby Smart's first year in Athens has not been particularly stunning. We lost a homecoming matchup against an extremely weak Vandy team. We had a close call against a poor FCS opponent, Nicholls State. Also, we had a last second nail biter win against a relatively weak Kentucky team. So it is obvious that SOMETHING needs to change.

This CHANGE will be seen in the next two or three years, due to one fact alone. This is a young team. The Dawgs have a pretty dominate defense, but it is also the youngest defense in the conference. And the offense is young also. In the table below I provided a list of young stars that will be leading our team to do something grat in the folowing years. These are some tough playmakers that I cannot wait to see in the years to come. Below are a few of the freshman stars to look out for with their statistics through the first 9 games.

  1. We have yet another stunning recruitment class for 2017
  2. The offensive line woes of this season will soon be over as a large freshman class is stepping in to compete
  3. Kirby Smart needs more than one year to turn the program around

Let's keep our heads up!! GO DAWGS!!
